Lasanga

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish of pasta sheets layered with fillings and baked. The word can refer to the pasta sheet itself or the baked dish.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Olive oil
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather the ingredients
  2. Step 2: Cook onion until translucent
  3. Step 3: Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes
  4. Step 4: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, remaining 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl until combined
  5. Step 5: Assemble lasagna
